About Me

My Story

Hello, I’m Arya Ankat, a first-year PhD student in Electrical Engineering at UC Riverside. I began my graduate journey at UCR in Fall 2023 as a master’s student, where I built a strong foundation in VLSI design and circuit reliability. During my master’s, I worked on processor design and studied the impact of aging on circuits by analyzing changes in power density and thermal maps before and after aging. To capture long-term reliability effects, we evaluated a worst-case aging scenario of 10 years. Building on that experience, my PhD work has expanded toward the software side of hardware design. I am currently working on machine learning for hardware, where I developed an agentic flow that can generate hardware code and automate the design flow from RTL-to-GDS.
