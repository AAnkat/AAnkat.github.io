About Me

My Story

Arya Ankat is a PhD researcher in Electrical Engineering at the University of California, Riverside, specializing at the intersection of hardware design and machine learning. With a master’s degree from UCR as a foundation, his research has spanned VLSI design, processor architecture, and circuit reliability — including the analysis of long-term aging effects on power density and thermal behavior. His current PhD work bridges the hardware-software boundary: he is developing an agentic AI flow capable of generating hardware description code and automating the full RTL-to-GDS design pipeline, advancing the state of intelligent hardware design automation.
